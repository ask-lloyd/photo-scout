# Sigma 24-70mm f/2.8 DG DN Art II

## Strengths
- Optical performance that rivals first-party GM and L-series lenses at a lower price
- Compact and well-balanced at 740g with premium build quality
- HLA (High-response Linear Actuator) autofocus for fast and silent operation
- Available in both Sony E-mount and Leica L-mount for cross-system flexibility

## Best For
- Professional and enthusiast photographers wanting top-tier standard zoom performance at a compelling price
- Sony and L-mount shooters seeking an alternative to more expensive first-party options
- Wedding and event photography where optical quality and reliability are non-negotiable
