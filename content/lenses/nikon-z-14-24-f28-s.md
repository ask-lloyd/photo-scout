# Nikon NIKKOR Z 14-24mm f/2.8 S

## Strengths
- Compact and lightweight design for an ultra-wide f/2.8 zoom at only 650g
- Accepts rear drop-in filters and optional front filter holder for 112mm filters
- Outstanding coma control and edge sharpness making it superb for astrophotography
- ARNEO and Nano Crystal Coat deliver exceptional flare resistance

## Best For
- Astrophotography and Milky Way shooting with superb point-light rendering
- Landscape photography requiring expansive ultra-wide perspectives
- Architecture and real estate photography with minimal distortion and outstanding sharpness
