## Strengths
- Ultra-fast f/1.2 aperture with remarkable sharpness even wide open
- Extraordinary bokeh quality with smooth, creamy background rendering

- Ring USM autofocus with full-time manual override for precise focusing

- Portrait photography with unmatched subject isolation and depth-of-field control
- Low-light and indoor photography where maximum light gathering is essential
- Photographers seeking the pinnacle of standard prime optical performance
