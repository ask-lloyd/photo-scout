# Tamron 17-28mm f/2.8 Di III RXD

## Strengths
- Extremely compact and lightweight for an ultra-wide f/2.8 zoom at just 420g
- Shares the 67mm filter thread with Tamron's 28-75mm and 70-180mm for convenience
- Very good sharpness and low distortion across the zoom range
- Internal zoom design with no extending barrel for better handling

## Best For
- Landscape and travel photography where ultrawide perspective is needed without bulk
- Vlogging and content creation benefiting from the wide angle and compact size
- Budget-friendly complement to the Tamron f/2.8 zoom trio for Sony shooters
