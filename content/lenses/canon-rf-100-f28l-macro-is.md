# Canon RF 100mm f/2.8L Macro IS USM

## Strengths
- 1.4x maximum magnification exceeds standard 1:1 macro for even greater close-up detail
- Unique SA Control ring lets you adjust the character of bokeh from smooth to swirly
- 5-stop optical image stabilization with hybrid IS for stable handheld macro shooting
- Outstanding sharpness as both a macro and a portrait lens

## Best For
- Macro and close-up photography with best-in-class magnification
- Portrait photography with the unique SA Control bokeh customization
- Product photography where fine detail rendering and flexibility are essential
