# Nikon NIKKOR Z 100-400mm f/4.5-5.6 VR S

## Strengths
- Excellent sharpness and contrast throughout the 4x zoom range with ED and Super ED elements
- 5.5-stop VR with Synchro VR support for enhanced stabilization with compatible bodies
- Compatible with Z teleconverters (1.4x and 2x) for reach up to 800mm
- Relatively compact and lightweight for the focal length range at 1435g

## Best For
- Wildlife and bird photography with versatile telephoto reach
- Outdoor sports photography where variable framing at distance is required
- Nature and travel photography as an all-in-one telephoto solution
