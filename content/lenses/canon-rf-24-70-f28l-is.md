# Canon RF 24-70mm f/2.8L IS USM

## Strengths
- Built-in 5-stop optical image stabilization, a first for a 24-70 f/2.8 zoom
- Excellent sharpness with three aspherical and three UD elements
- Customizable control ring for direct adjustment of aperture, ISO, or exposure compensation
- Nano USM autofocus delivers fast, smooth, and silent focusing for stills and video

## Best For
- Professional event and wedding photography with the advantage of in-lens IS
- Canon RF system shooters who need a versatile, top-tier standard zoom
- Hybrid photo-video work where stabilization and silent focusing are valuable
