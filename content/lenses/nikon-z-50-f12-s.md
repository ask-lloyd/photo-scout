# Nikon NIKKOR Z 50mm f/1.2 S

## Strengths
- Razor-sharp wide open at f/1.2 with no compromise on resolution
- Gorgeous, silky bokeh with perfectly rounded highlights from 9-blade diaphragm
- Multi-focus system provides fast, accurate, and silent autofocus
- ARNEO and Nano Crystal Coat for pristine contrast and flare resistance

## Best For
- Portrait photography with extreme shallow depth of field and dreamy backgrounds
- Low-light photography where maximum light-gathering capability is paramount
- Photographers seeking the ultimate in standard prime rendering on the Nikon Z system
