# Tamron 28-75mm f/2.8 Di III VXD G2

## Strengths
- Outstanding image quality that rivals lenses costing twice as much
- Lightweight and compact at 540g with a small 67mm filter size
- VXD linear motor autofocus is fast, accurate, and nearly silent
- Excellent close-focusing ability with 0.18m minimum focus distance at the wide end

## Best For
- Budget-conscious photographers who want f/2.8 zoom quality without the premium price
- Travel photography where weight savings and versatility are priorities
- Everyday walk-around lens for Sony full-frame shooters
