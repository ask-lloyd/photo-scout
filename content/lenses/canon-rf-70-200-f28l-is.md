# Canon RF 70-200mm f/2.8L IS USM

## Strengths
- Compact non-extending internal zoom design that is shorter than traditional 70-200 f/2.8 lenses
- 5.5-stop optical stabilization combined with IBIS-equipped bodies for even greater effectiveness
- Outstanding sharpness from center to corner with Super UD and UD elements
- Dual Nano USM motors for fast, silent autofocus ideal for both stills and video

## Best For
- Sports, wildlife, and action photography requiring fast autofocus and stabilization
- Portrait and event photography with beautiful bokeh at f/2.8
- Travel-friendly professional telephoto thanks to its compact design
