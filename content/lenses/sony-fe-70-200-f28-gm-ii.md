# Sony FE 70-200mm f/2.8 GM II

## Strengths
- Class-leading sharpness with XA element and ED glass throughout the zoom range
- Dramatically lighter than competing 70-200 f/2.8 lenses at 1045g
- Outstanding 7-stop image stabilization for handheld shooting at slow shutter speeds
- Blazing fast quad XD linear motor AF system with excellent tracking

## Best For
- Sports, action, and wildlife photography requiring fast aperture and reach
- Portrait photography with beautiful bokeh from 70-200mm at f/2.8
- Event and wedding photography where versatility and low-light performance are critical
