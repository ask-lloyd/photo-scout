# Sigma 14mm f/1.4 DG DN Art

## Strengths
- World's widest f/1.4 rectilinear autofocus lens, purpose-built for astrophotography
- Exceptional coma and sagittal flare correction for pinpoint stars to the corners
- Rear filter holder accepts standard gelatin filters for creative flexibility
- Outstanding sharpness wide open at f/1.4 across the entire frame

## Best For
- Astrophotography and Milky Way capture where maximum light gathering and coma control are critical
- Landscape photography at night or in extreme low-light conditions
- Creative wide-angle photography with unprecedented shallow depth of field at 14mm
