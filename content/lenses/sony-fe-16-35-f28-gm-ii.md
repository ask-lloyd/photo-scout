# Sony FE 16-35mm f/2.8 GM II

## Strengths
- Remarkable corner-to-corner sharpness even at the widest 16mm setting
- Extremely lightweight for a fast ultra-wide zoom at just 547g
- Minimal distortion and excellent coma performance for astrophotography
- Close minimum focusing distance of 0.22m at the wide end

## Best For
- Landscape and architectural photography demanding wide perspectives with top image quality
- Astrophotography and night sky shooting with f/2.8 and low coma
- Real estate and interior photography where ultra-wide coverage is essential
