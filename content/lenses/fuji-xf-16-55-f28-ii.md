# Fujifilm XF 16-55mm f/2.8 R LM WR II

- Linear motor autofocus provides fast, quiet, and precise focusing

- Fujifilm X-series shooters who need a professional-grade standard zoom without bulk
- Travel and street photography where weight and size matter
- Event and wedding photography on the Fujifilm system
