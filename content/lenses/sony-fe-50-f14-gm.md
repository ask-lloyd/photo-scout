# Sony FE 50mm f/1.4 GM

## Strengths
- Tack sharp wide open at f/1.4 with beautifully smooth bokeh from XA elements
- Remarkably light and compact for a 50mm f/1.4 at only 516g
- Near-silent XD linear motor AF with virtually zero focus breathing
- Excellent 11-blade circular aperture for pleasing out-of-focus highlights

## Best For
- Portrait photography with stunning subject isolation and bokeh
- Low-light photography and indoor events where fast aperture is critical
- Everyday prime lens for photographers who value optical excellence and portability
