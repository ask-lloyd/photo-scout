# Tamron 70-180mm f/2.8 Di III VXD G2

## Strengths
- Remarkably compact and lightweight for a 70-180mm f/2.8 at only 855g
- Improved optical performance in G2 with enhanced sharpness and reduced aberrations
- Small 67mm filter thread matches other Tamron Di III zooms for filter sharing
- VXD linear motor autofocus with excellent tracking performance

## Best For
- Portrait and event photographers wanting f/2.8 telephoto reach without the weight
- Sports and action photography on a budget with fast aperture capability
- Travel photographers who need telephoto reach in a compact, lightweight package
