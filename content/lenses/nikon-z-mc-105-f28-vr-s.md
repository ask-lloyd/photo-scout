# Nikon NIKKOR Z MC 105mm f/2.8 VR S

## Strengths
- True 1:1 macro reproduction with outstanding sharpness at all focusing distances
- 4.5-stop optical VR stabilization crucial for handheld macro work
- Multi-focus system enables fast and accurate autofocus even at close distances
- Beautiful rendering as a portrait lens with pleasing bokeh from the 9-blade diaphragm

## Best For
- Macro photography of insects, flowers, and small products with true 1:1 reproduction
- Portrait photography with flattering 105mm focal length and smooth background blur
- Scientific and product photography where precise detail and accurate color are required
