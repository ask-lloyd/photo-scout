# Sigma 100-400mm f/5-6.3 DG DN OS Contemporary

## Strengths
- Lightweight (1135g) super-telephoto zoom that is practical to handhold all day
- 4-stop Optical Stabilization for sharp handheld shots at 400mm
- Fast and quiet stepping-motor autofocus with full-time manual override
- Excellent value for reach — delivers near-prime sharpness across the zoom range

## Best For
- Wildlife and bird photography where portability matters as much as reach
- Travel telephoto work, motorsport, and airshows
- Sony E and L-mount shooters wanting 400mm without the first-party price tag
