# Fujifilm XF 23mm f/1.4 R LM WR

## Strengths
- Classic 35mm equivalent field of view with fast f/1.4 aperture
- Linear motor autofocus is dramatically faster and quieter than the original version
- Weather-resistant construction for reliable use in challenging conditions
- Excellent sharpness and rendering with pleasing bokeh characteristics

## Best For
- Street and documentary photography with the classic 35mm perspective
- Everyday walk-around prime for Fujifilm X-series photographers
- Low-light shooting where the wide f/1.4 aperture provides critical flexibility
