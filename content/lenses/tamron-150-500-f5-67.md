# Tamron 150-500mm f/5-6.7 Di III VC VXD

## Strengths
- Excellent super-telephoto reach up to 500mm at a fraction of the cost of first-party alternatives
- 4-stop VC image stabilization for practical handheld shooting at long focal lengths
- VXD linear motor autofocus delivers fast and precise focus acquisition
- Surprisingly good optical quality with LD and XLD elements controlling chromatic aberration

## Best For
- Wildlife and bird photography on a budget with impressive 500mm reach
- Plane spotting and motorsport photography from spectator areas
- Nature photographers seeking affordable super-telephoto capability for the Sony system
