# Sony FE 200-600mm f/5.6-6.3 G

## Strengths
- Internal zoom design maintains consistent length and balance throughout the zoom range
- Impressive sharpness across the entire 200-600mm range with ED and aspherical elements
- 5-stop optical image stabilization enables handheld use even at 600mm
- Compatible with 1.4x and 2x teleconverters for reach up to 1200mm

## Best For
- Wildlife and bird photography where long reach with versatile framing is essential
- Airshow and motorsport photography requiring variable super-telephoto focal lengths
- Budget-conscious photographers seeking premium super-telephoto reach without prime lens cost
