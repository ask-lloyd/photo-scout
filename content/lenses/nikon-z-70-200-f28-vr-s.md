# Nikon NIKKOR Z 70-200mm f/2.8 VR S

## Strengths
- Exceptional optical performance with multi-focusing system for sharpness throughout the range
- 5.5-stop VR with Synchro VR support for up to 6 stops combined with IBIS bodies
- Smooth and natural bokeh from the 9-blade rounded diaphragm
- Robust magnesium alloy build with full weather sealing and fluorine-coated front element

## Best For
- Sports and action photography requiring fast focusing and reliable stabilization
- Portrait and wedding photography with flattering telephoto compression and bokeh
- Professional Nikon Z shooters needing the essential telephoto workhorse
