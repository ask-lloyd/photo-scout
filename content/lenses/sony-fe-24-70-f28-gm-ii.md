# Sony FE 24-70mm f/2.8 GM II

## Strengths
- Exceptionally sharp across the entire zoom range, even wide open at f/2.8
- Significantly lighter and more compact than its predecessor at just 695g
- Fast and precise XD linear motor autofocus with minimal focus breathing
- Excellent flare and ghosting resistance with Nano AR Coating II

## Best For
- Professional event, wedding, and portrait photography
- Everyday walk-around lens for full-frame Sony shooters who demand top optical quality
- Video production where compact size and smooth focus transitions matter
