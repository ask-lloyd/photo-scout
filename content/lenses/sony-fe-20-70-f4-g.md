# Sony FE 20-70mm f/4 G

## Strengths
- Unique 20-70mm range offers significantly wider coverage than traditional 24-70mm zooms
- Lightweight and compact at 488g, making it an excellent travel companion
- Very good sharpness across the frame with two AA and two ED elements
- Smooth and quiet XD linear motor autofocus with minimal focus breathing

## Best For
- Travel and everyday photography where the extra wide angle at 20mm adds creative flexibility
- Content creators and vloggers who benefit from the wider field of view
- Photographers who want a versatile one-lens solution without carrying a separate ultra-wide
