# Canon RF 15-35mm f/2.8L IS USM

## Strengths
- 5-stop optical image stabilization uncommon in ultra-wide zooms for handheld flexibility
- Front filter thread (82mm) instead of bulbous front element, allowing standard filter use
- Outstanding edge-to-edge sharpness with three aspherical and two UD elements
- Weather-sealed L-series build quality for demanding outdoor conditions

## Best For
- Landscape photography where wide perspective and filter compatibility are both needed
- Architecture and interior photography requiring ultra-wide coverage with low distortion
- Vlogging and video production benefiting from stabilization at wide angles
