# Sony FE 85mm f/1.4 GM

## Strengths
- Exceptional rendering with creamy, three-dimensional bokeh from XA element
- Outstanding sharpness at f/1.4 with smooth transition to out-of-focus areas
- 11-blade circular aperture produces perfectly round highlights
- Reliable and fast direct-drive SSM autofocus with focus hold button

## Best For
- Dedicated portrait photography with unmatched background separation
- Wedding and fashion photography requiring dreamy, flattering rendering
- Studio and environmental portraiture where 85mm provides natural perspective
