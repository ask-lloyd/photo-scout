# Canon RF 100-500mm f/4.5-7.1L IS USM

## Strengths
- Massive 5x zoom range from 100-500mm in a relatively portable package
- 6-stop optical image stabilization for reliable handheld shooting at extreme focal lengths
- Compatible with RF 1.4x and 2x extenders for reach up to 1000mm
- L-series build quality with comprehensive weather sealing

## Best For
- Wildlife and bird photography requiring versatile long reach without lens changes
- Outdoor sports photography from sideline or spectator positions
- Travel-oriented nature photography where carrying multiple telephoto primes is impractical
