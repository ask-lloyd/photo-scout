# Fujifilm X100VI

## Strengths

- Fixed 23mm f/2 lens (35mm equivalent) is razor-sharp and perfectly matched to the 40.2MP sensor
- Hybrid optical/electronic viewfinder offers a unique shooting experience not found on any competitor
- IBIS in this compact fixed-lens body is a first for the X100 series, enabling slower shutter speeds
- Fujifilm's film simulation modes produce stunning in-camera JPEGs beloved by street photographers
- Compact, pocketable design with retro styling makes it the ultimate discreet street camera

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 125-400 — exceptional detail from the high-resolution sensor
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 125-3200 — noise well-controlled for standard prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 125-6400 — usable with light noise reduction for digital sharing
