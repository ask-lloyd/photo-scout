# Canon R8

## Strengths

- Full-frame image quality in one of the lightest and most affordable full-frame bodies available
- Outstanding high-ISO performance makes it excellent for available-light and event photography
- 40fps electronic shutter burst rate punches well above its price class
- Same advanced Dual Pixel CMOS AF II system found in Canon's professional bodies
- 4K 60p video with Canon Log 3 and oversampled output delivers strong video quality

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-1600 — clean output with excellent detail
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-12800 — manageable noise for standard prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-25600 — noise is acceptable for screen-sized delivery
