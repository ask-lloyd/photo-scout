# Canon R5 Mark II

## Strengths

- 45MP stacked CMOS sensor combines high resolution with 30fps electronic shutter and minimal rolling shutter
- Industry-leading 8.5-stop IBIS enables sharp handheld shots in extremely low light
- Dual Pixel Intelligent AF with eye control AF allows intuitive focus point selection
- 8K 60p raw internal recording pushes video capabilities to professional cinema territory
- Excellent balance of resolution and speed makes it viable for both studio and action work

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-800 — clean, detail-rich files
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-6400 — strong detail retention for large prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-12800 — noise remains manageable for screen-sized output
