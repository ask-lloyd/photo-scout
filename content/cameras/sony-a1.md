# Sony A1

## Strengths

- 30fps blackout-free continuous shooting with full AF/AE tracking is unmatched for action photography
- 50.1MP sensor delivers high resolution without compromising speed — the best of both worlds
- Anti-distortion stacked CMOS sensor virtually eliminates rolling shutter artifacts
- Advanced real-time tracking AF with eye detection for humans, animals, and birds
- 8K 30p and 4K 120p video capabilities make it a true do-everything flagship

- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-6400 — excellent quality for large prints
