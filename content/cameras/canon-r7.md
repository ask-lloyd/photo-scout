# Canon R7

## Strengths

- 1.6x crop factor combined with 32.5MP delivers exceptional reach for wildlife and sports
- 30fps electronic shutter with full tracking AF captures rapid action sequences
- 8-stop IBIS provides excellent stabilization for handheld telephoto shooting
- High resolution APS-C sensor allows aggressive cropping while retaining usable detail
- Access to Canon's full RF lens ecosystem including affordable RF-S lenses

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-400 — maximum detail with minimal noise
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-3200 — acceptable noise for standard print sizes
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-6400 — usable with noise reduction for screen output
