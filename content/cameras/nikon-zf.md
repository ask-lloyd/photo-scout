# Nikon Zf

## Strengths

- Heritage-inspired design with dedicated analog dials for shutter speed, ISO, and exposure compensation
- Same excellent 24.5MP sensor as the Z6 III with outstanding low-light performance
- 8-stop IBIS enables remarkably slow handheld shutter speeds for creative shooting
- Pixel-shift shooting mode can produce 96MP composites for detailed still-life and landscape work
- Compact and stylish body appeals to street and travel photographers seeking a less conspicuous camera

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-1600 — clean, detailed output
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-12800 — noise well-managed for standard and large prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-25600 — high-ISO performance remains strong for screen output
