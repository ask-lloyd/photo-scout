# Sony A7C II

## Strengths

- Compact rangefinder-style body makes full-frame quality highly portable for travel and street photography
- Same 33MP sensor and AI-based AF system as higher-end models in a smaller form factor
- 7-stop IBIS is remarkably effective for a body this small
- Full-frame image quality with excellent dynamic range in a body rivaling APS-C cameras in size
- 4K 60p video with S-Log3 and S-Cinetone profiles for serious video work

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-800 — clean, detailed files
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-6400 — good detail retention for standard print sizes
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-12800 — noise is manageable for screen output
