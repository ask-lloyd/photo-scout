# Nikon Z8

## Strengths

- Class-leading 14.8 EV dynamic range at base ISO 64 is exceptional for landscape and high-contrast work
- 45.7MP stacked CMOS sensor delivers Z9 performance in a smaller, more portable body
- 20fps with full AF tracking and minimal rolling shutter handles demanding action photography
- Dual card slots (CFexpress Type B and SD UHS-II) provide flexible and reliable storage
- 8K 60p and 4K 120p internal recording make it a serious video production tool

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 64-400 — extraordinary detail and dynamic range
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 64-6400 — excellent quality for large format prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 64-12800 — noise well-managed for digital delivery
