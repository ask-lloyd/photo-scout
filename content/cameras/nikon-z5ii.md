# Nikon Z5 II

## Strengths

- Affordable full-frame entry point with strong image quality and reliable autofocus
- 7-stop IBIS provides excellent handheld stabilization for a camera in this price range
- 14fps burst rate is a significant upgrade over its predecessor for casual action shooting
- Dual SD card slots offer flexible and redundant storage for important shoots
- Solid build quality with weather sealing makes it suitable for outdoor photography

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-800 — clean files with strong detail
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-6400 — noise well-controlled for standard prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-12800 — usable high-ISO output for screen delivery
