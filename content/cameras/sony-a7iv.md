# Sony A7 IV

## Strengths

- Versatile 33MP sensor balances resolution and file size for a wide range of photography
- Excellent 4K 60p video with 10-bit 4:2:2 color makes it a strong hybrid camera
- Real-time eye AF for humans, animals, and birds ensures reliable subject tracking
- Outstanding dynamic range of 14.7 EV handles challenging lighting conditions
- Extensive native Sony E-mount lens ecosystem with excellent options at every price point

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-800 — clean files with full detail retention
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-6400 — noise remains well-controlled for standard prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-12800 — usable results with moderate noise reduction
