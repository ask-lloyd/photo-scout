# Sony A6700

## Strengths

- Compact APS-C body with AI-based AF inherited from full-frame flagships
- 5-stop IBIS in a small form factor provides handheld stability unusual for APS-C cameras
- Excellent 4K 60p video with S-Log3 and S-Cinetone makes it a capable video platform
- 1.5x crop factor turns affordable telephoto lenses into strong wildlife and sports options
- Front-facing articulating screen and clean HDMI output suit content creators

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-400 — maximum detail with minimal noise
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-3200 — noise well-managed for standard print sizes
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-6400 — usable with moderate noise reduction applied
