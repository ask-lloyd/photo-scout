# Canon R6 III

## Strengths

- Exceptional low-light performance with usable results up to ISO 25600 for available-light shooting
- 40fps electronic shutter with full AF tracking captures decisive moments in fast action
- 8-stop IBIS enables sharp handheld shots in very challenging lighting conditions
- 24.2MP keeps file sizes manageable while providing more than enough resolution for most uses
- Advanced Dual Pixel CMOS AF II with deep learning subject detection across the full frame

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-1600 — very clean files with excellent detail
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-12800 — noise well-controlled for standard and large prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-25600 — usable even at very high sensitivity for screen output
