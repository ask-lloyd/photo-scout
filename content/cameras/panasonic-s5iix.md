# Panasonic S5 IIX

## Strengths

- Phase-detect autofocus is a major leap forward for Panasonic, delivering fast and reliable subject tracking
- Unlimited 6K 30p and 5.9K 30p recording with internal ProRes and All-Intra codecs for professional video
- 6.5-stop IBIS with Active I.S. mode provides exceptional handheld video stabilization
- Real-time LUT application allows in-camera color grading for streamlined video workflows
- Leica L-mount alliance provides access to lenses from Panasonic, Sigma, and Leica

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-800 — clean files with strong detail
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-6400 — noise well-managed for standard prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-12800 — acceptable noise for screen-sized output
