# Sony A7R V

## Strengths

- Exceptional 61MP resolution delivers extraordinary detail for large prints and heavy cropping
- AI-based autofocus with dedicated processing unit provides superior subject recognition and tracking
- 8-stop IBIS allows handheld shooting at remarkably slow shutter speeds
- Outstanding dynamic range of 14.7 EV preserves highlight and shadow detail in high-contrast scenes
- Pixel-shift multi-shot mode can produce 240MP composites for ultimate resolution on a tripod

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-400 — full detail with negligible noise
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-3200 — maintains excellent detail for large format output
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-12800 — noise is well-controlled for screen-sized output
