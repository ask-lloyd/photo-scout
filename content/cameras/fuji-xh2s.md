# Fujifilm X-H2S

## Strengths

- 40fps electronic shutter with full AF tracking makes it the fastest APS-C camera for action
- Stacked BSI CMOS sensor virtually eliminates rolling shutter for distortion-free electronic shutter use
- 6.2K 30p and 4K 120p internal recording with ProRes and F-Log2 support for professional video
- 1.5x crop factor extends telephoto reach, ideal for wildlife and sports on a budget
- Dual CFexpress Type B and SD card slots handle the high data rates of burst shooting

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 160-800 — clean files with full detail
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 160-3200 — noise manageable for standard print sizes
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 160-6400 — usable output for screen-sized delivery
