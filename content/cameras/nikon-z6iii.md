# Nikon Z6 III

## Strengths

- Partially stacked CMOS sensor provides fast readout with excellent low-light performance
- Outstanding high-ISO capability makes it ideal for event, astro, and available-light photography
- 20fps burst with full AF tracking handles action photography with confidence
- 6K raw internal recording and 4K 120p make it one of the best hybrid cameras available
- 8-stop IBIS enables handheld shooting in extremely challenging lighting conditions

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 100-1600 — clean, detailed files
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 100-12800 — excellent noise control for all print sizes
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 100-25600 — high sensitivity remains usable for screen output
