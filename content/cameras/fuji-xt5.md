# Fujifilm X-T5

## Strengths

- 40.2MP X-Trans CMOS 5 HR sensor delivers exceptional resolution for an APS-C camera
- Classic rangefinder-style body with dedicated analog dials for a tactile shooting experience
- Fujifilm's renowned film simulation modes produce beautiful in-camera JPEG color science
- 7-stop IBIS provides excellent handheld stabilization for this resolution class
- Three-way tilting screen favors stills photography over the flip-out screens common on competitors

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 125-400 — maximum detail with Fuji's distinctive color
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 125-3200 — noise well-controlled for large prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 125-6400 — usable output with moderate noise reduction
