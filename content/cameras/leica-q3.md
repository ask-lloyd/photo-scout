# Leica Q3

## Strengths

- Fixed Summilux 28mm f/1.7 lens is exceptionally sharp and fast, perfectly matched to the 60.3MP sensor
- 60.3MP resolution enables digital crop modes at 35mm, 50mm, 75mm, and 90mm with usable output
- Macro mode with a minimum focus distance of 17cm adds close-up versatility to a fixed-lens camera
- Phase-detect autofocus with subject tracking is fast and reliable across the frame
- Premium Leica build quality, handling, and image rendering in a self-contained package

## Settings Notes

- **Best quality ISO range:** ISO 50-400 — extraordinary detail with beautiful rendering
- **Acceptable for print:** ISO 50-6400 — strong detail retention for large prints
- **Acceptable for web:** ISO 50-12800 — noise well-managed for screen-sized output
