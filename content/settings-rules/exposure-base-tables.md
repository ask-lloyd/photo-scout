# Exposure Value Reference Tables

Exposure Value (EV) is a single number representing a combination of shutter speed and aperture that produces a given exposure at a specific ISO. Understanding EV values for common lighting conditions allows you to quickly estimate correct exposure without metering, verify your meter readings, and anticipate the settings you will need before arriving at a location.

## EV Values by Lighting Condition (ISO 100)

| EV | Lighting Condition | Example Scenario |
|----|--------------------|--------------------|
| 16 | Bright sand or snow | White sand beach in full midday sun |
| 15 | Bright direct sunlight | Sunny 16 rule: f/16, 1/ISO sec |
| 14 | Hazy sun, soft shadows | Light overcast with visible sun |
| 13 | Bright cloudy, no shadows | Overcast but bright white sky |
| 12 | Overcast | Heavy cloud cover, even illumination |
| 11 | Heavy overcast or open shade | Deep shade on a bright day |
| 10 | Twilight, shade | Open shade on an overcast day |
| 9 | Sunset on horizon | Sun touching the horizon line |
| 8 | Late sunset / early sunrise | Sun just below horizon, vivid color |
| 7 | Civil twilight | After sunset, sky still bright |
| 5 | Blue hour | Deep blue sky, city lights visible |
| 4 | Late blue hour | Transition to darkness, lights dominant |
| 2 | Full darkness with city glow | Night in a suburban area |
| 0 | Moonless night, rural | Dark sky location, stars visible |
| -2 | Starlight only | Remote dark sky, no artificial light |
| -4 | Milky Way core detail | Deep sky, faint nebulae |

## The Sunny 16 Rule

In bright, direct sunlight, the correct exposure at f/16 is a shutter speed equal to 1/ISO. This provides a simple mental starting point for any outdoor exposure calculation.

| ISO | f/16 Shutter | f/11 Shutter | f/8 Shutter | f/5.6 Shutter | f/4 Shutter |
|-----|-------------|-------------|------------|--------------|------------|
| 100 | 1/100 | 1/200 | 1/400 | 1/800 | 1/1600 |
| 200 | 1/200 | 1/400 | 1/800 | 1/1600 | 1/3200 |
| 400 | 1/400 | 1/800 | 1/1600 | 1/3200 | 1/6400 |
| 800 | 1/800 | 1/1600 | 1/3200 | 1/6400 | 1/8000 |

## EV Adjustments from Sunny 16

| Condition Change | EV Adjustment |
|-----------------|---------------|
| Bright sand/snow | +1 EV |
| Hazy sun (soft shadows) | -1 EV |
| Cloudy bright (no shadows) | -2 EV |
| Heavy overcast | -3 EV |
| Open shade | -4 EV |
| Backlit subject | -2 to -3 EV |
| Sunset at horizon | -6 EV |
| Blue hour | -10 EV |

## Equivalent Exposure Combinations (EV 15, ISO 100)

All of these combinations produce the same exposure in bright sunlight:

| Aperture | Shutter Speed | Use Case |
|----------|--------------|----------|
| f/1.4 | 1/32000 | Ultra-fast action freeze |
| f/2 | 1/16000 | Shallow DOF in bright light |
| f/2.8 | 1/8000 | Sports with subject separation |
| f/4 | 1/4000 | General fast action |
| f/5.6 | 1/2000 | Portraits with blurred background |
| f/8 | 1/1000 | Sharp general purpose |
| f/11 | 1/500 | Landscape, good sharpness |
| f/16 | 1/250 | Deep DOF landscape |
| f/22 | 1/125 | Maximum DOF (diffraction risk) |
