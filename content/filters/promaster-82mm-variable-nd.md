# Promaster 82mm Variable ND (1–9 stops)

## Strengths
- Single filter covers everything from light ND up to long-exposure territory
- Lets you shoot wide-aperture portraits in bright sun without overexposing
- Enables motion blur on waterfalls, rivers, and clouds mid-day

## Best For
- Long-exposure landscape work (silky water, streaky clouds)
- Video at 1/50s shutter in bright sunlight
- Use with 82mm-threaded zooms like the Sony FE 24-70 GM II or step-up ring from 77mm
