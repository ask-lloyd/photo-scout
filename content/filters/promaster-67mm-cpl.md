# Promaster 67mm Circular Polarizer

## Strengths
- Cuts glare from water, glass, and wet foliage for deeper, more saturated color
- Deepens blue skies and boosts contrast between clouds and sky
- Multi-coated glass keeps color cast and light loss minimal

## Best For
- Landscape and nature photography, especially around water or leaves
- Travel shots where sky saturation and reflection control matter
- Pair with wide-to-standard zooms (e.g., Sigma 24-70, Sony 24-70 GM)
